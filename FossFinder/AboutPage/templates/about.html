About FOSS

Free and open-source software(FOSS) is software available to the public for use, modification and distribution. FOSS fosters collaboration and long-term sustainability. FOSS also plays a significant role because it's cost effective, flexible, innovative, secure and easily accessible.


Existing Solutions

Websites such as FOSSHub try to find open source software by providing a centeralized resource for information and downloading popular forms of FOSS. However, the limitation to this is that FOSSHub only maintains listings for a relatively small number of programs which fit a critera and focusing on the popular programs at that moment in time.


Who We Are

The people behind this project are college students that want to provide a useful resource to our peers. We understand how important it is to on having free software as we pay for education. There are plenty of resources online to find free open software but with those, most of them, compare each listing to a paid version which isn't what we're looking for.
